-R, --recursive
list subdirectories recursively
(List files and directories recursively, including subdirectories.)

-l
use a long listing format
(detailed information about files and directories)

-a, --all
do not ignore entries starting with . 
(hidden files and directories)

-r, --reverse
reverse order while sorting (non-alphabetically)

-t
sort by time, newest first; see --time
(displaying the most recently modified ones first)
